## Step 1: Create an Album and Add a Memory

1. Launch the **MemoriaR** app and tap the **"+"** button on the Home screen.
2. Enter a name for your album and confirm.
3. Open the album you made in step 2, then tap **"+"** again to add a memory.
4. Select a video from your library.

![Add Album](/images/tech-blog/2026-memoriar-quick-start-guide-en/add-album.avif)
![Add Memory](/images/tech-blog/2026-memoriar-quick-start-guide-en/add-memory.avif)

---

## Step 2: Print Your Memory

Printing is what makes the AR magic possible — MemoriaR recognizes your **physical print** to trigger the video.

1. Tap on the memory you added to open the Memory screen.
2. Tap the **Print** button and follow the on-screen prompts.

Make sure you have a color printer available, or use a local print service.

![Print Memory](/images/tech-blog/2026-memoriar-quick-start-guide-en/print-memory.avif)

---

## Step 3: Watch It Come to Life

1. Back on the Album screen, tap the **Play** button to start your AR session.
2. Hold your iPhone/iPad camera over the **printed photo** in good lighting.
3. MemoriaR will recognize the image — and your video will appear right on top of the photo.

![AR Session](/images/tech-blog/2026-memoriar-quick-start-guide-en/ar-session.gif)

_The actual experience is much sharper._

## Tips for the Best Experience

- **Lighting matters** — scan in a well-lit environment for faster recognition.
